# üé≤ Buy/Place Betting Guide - Rollers Paradise

## Overview
Buy and Place bets are wagers on specific numbers (2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12) that win when that number is rolled, and lose when a 7 is rolled.

---

## üîÑ Accessing Buy/Place Bets Mode

1. Look at the **left column** of the craps table
2. Find the **"SWITCH TO BUY/PLACE"** button (below "Bonus Bets Working")
3. Click it to toggle between:
   - **Bonus Bets Mode** (Hardways, Hops, Propositions)
   - **Buy/Place Bets Mode** (Number betting interface)

---

## üí∞ Buy Bets vs Place Bets

### **BUY BETS** (Green button)
- ‚úÖ **Pay TRUE ODDS** - Best possible payouts
- ‚ùå **5% Commission** - House takes 5% vig on your bet
- üìä **Best for**: 4, 10 (and crapless numbers 2, 3, 11, 12)

**Payouts:**
```
2 & 12:  6:1  (Best odds!)
3 & 11:  3:1
4 & 10:  2:1
5 & 9:   3:2
6 & 8:   6:5
```

### **PLACE BETS** (Blue button)
- ‚úÖ **No Commission** - No vig charged
- ‚ùå **Slightly lower payouts** - House edge in the odds
- üìä **Best for**: 5, 6, 8, 9

**Payouts:**
```
2 & 12:  11:2
3 & 11:  11:4
4 & 10:  9:5
5 & 9:   7:5
6 & 8:   7:6
```

## üéØ How to Place Buy/Place Bets

### Step-by-Step:
1. **Switch to Buy/Place Mode** - Click "SWITCH TO BUY/PLACE" button
2. **Choose Bet Type**:
   - Click **"PLACE"** button for Place bets (no commission)
   - Click **"BUY"** button for Buy bets (5% commission)
3. **Select Your Chip** - Choose chip denomination from chip selector
4. **Click a Number** - Click any number box (2-12) to place bet
5. **Add More Bets** - Click same number again to add more chips
6. **Remove Bets** - Right-click any number to remove your bet

### Visual Indicators:
- **Red numbers (2, 3, 11, 12)** = Crapless craps extreme numbers
- **Blue numbers (4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10)** = Standard place numbers
- **Chips appear** on numbers when bets are placed
- **Payout odds** shown below each number

## üìà When Buy/Place Bets Win or Lose

### ‚úÖ **WIN Condition:**
- Your number is rolled **while bets are working**
- You get your **original bet BACK + winnings**
- Bet **stays on the table** for next roll

### ‚ùå **LOSE Condition:**
- **7 is rolled** while bets are working
- Bet is **removed from table**
- Must place new bet to continue

### ‚è∏Ô∏è **Bets Not Working:**
- During **Come Out Roll** (default)
- When **Bonus Bets Working is OFF**
- Numbers can roll without affecting your bets

## üéì Strategy Tips

### **For Beginners:**
- Start with **Place 6 and Place 8** (best house edge for place bets)
- Use **"BET ACROSS"** button to bet all numbers at once
- Keep bets small until you understand the game

### **For Advanced Players:**
- **Buy 4 and 10** instead of placing them (better odds even with vig)
- **Place 6 and 8** instead of buying (commission makes buy worse)
- In crapless craps, **Buy 2, 3, 11, 12** for huge payouts (but rare hits)

### **Optimal Strategy:**
```
BUY:   4, 10, 2, 3, 11, 12  (True odds worth the commission)
PLACE: 5, 6, 8, 9            (Small house edge, no vig)
```

## üî¢ Payout Examples

### Example 1: Buy 4 for $20

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Rules

### Working vs Not Working:
- Buy/Place bets are **OFF by default** on come out roll
- They activate (**turn ON**) when point is established
- They deactivate (**turn OFF**) when point is made or seven-out
- You can **manually toggle** with "Bonus Bets Working" button

### Multi-Player:
- All players' bets **stack on same numbers**
- See **chip stacks** when multiple players bet same number
- Your bets are **highlighted** with your player name
- Each player wins/loses **independently**

### Bet Minimums:
- Same as table minimum ($1 default)
- Commission rounded up to nearest cent
- Can bet multiple chips on same number

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between Buy and Place?**
A: Buy pays true odds but charges 5% commission. Place has no commission but slightly worse odds.

**Q: Which is better?**
A: Buy is better for 4, 10, 2, 3, 11, 12. Place is better for 5, 6, 8, 9.

**Q: Do bets work on the come out roll?**
A: No, they're OFF by default. They turn ON when point is established.

**Q: What happens if I place both Buy 6 AND Place 6?**
A: Both bets are separate. Both will win if 6 rolls, but Buy pays better odds minus vig.

**Q: Can I remove my bet after placing it?**
A: Yes! Right-click the number to remove your bet anytime before the roll.

**Q: Why are 2, 3, 11, 12 highlighted in red?**
A: These are crapless craps extreme numbers - very rare but HUGE payouts (especially with Buy bets).

**Q: Does "BET ACROSS" place Buy or Place bets?**
A: It uses smart defaults: Buy for 4, 10 and Place for 5, 6, 8, 9.

**Q: Can I use $500 and $1000 chips for Buy/Place bets?**
A: YES! All chip denominations work ($1, $5, $10, $25, $50, $100, $500, $1000).
